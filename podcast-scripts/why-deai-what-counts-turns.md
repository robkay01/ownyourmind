## TURN 14 — RACHEL
```
Three categories. AI as a participant in the game -- arbitrage bots on DEXs are AI using crypto infrastructure. Legitimate but doesn't make the DEX an AI project. AI as the objective of the game -- the blockchain exists specifically to build, train, or distribute AI. Bit-tensor's subnets are this. The token incentives exist to produce intelligence. And the problematic one -- AI as marketing for the game. The blockchain has its own purpose and AI was added to the narrative without changing the underlying mechanism.
```

But the distinction matters for understanding what you're buying.
