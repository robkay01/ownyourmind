## TURN 03 — BOBSKI

```
How does the inference synthesis work?
```

## TURN 04 — RACHEL
```
The network is organised around Topics -- compartmentalised sub-networks targeting specific prediction problems. Bitcoin price prediction, yield optimisation, DeFi risk assessment. Each Topic has workers who deploy ML models, forecast workers who predict accuracy of other workers' outputs, reputers who evaluate against ground truth data, and validators who secure the chain.

The novel mechanism is regret-based weighting. Inferences get weights based on whether they outperform previously calculated predictions. This prevents initially successful predictors from gaining disproportionate influence and lets the system continuously improve.
```
