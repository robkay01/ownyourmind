What problem does federated learning solve?
```

## TURN 04 — RACHEL
```
Most AI training requires centralising data -- a privacy, sovereignty, and regulatory nightmare. Federated learning trains models where the data lives. Only model updates, the gradients, are transmitted. The training data never leaves the participant's device. FLock adds blockchain incentives -- staking, slashing, rewards -- to make this economically viable and verifiable.
