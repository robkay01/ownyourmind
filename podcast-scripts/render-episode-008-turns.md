What does Render actually do?
```

## TURN 04 — RACHEL
```
Render is a GPU rendering marketplace on Solana. Creators and studios submit rendering jobs through OctaneRender, Blender Cycles, or the Render Portal. Jobs get encrypted, matched to node operators based on benchmarks and reputation, and output is validated through automated quality checks plus manual creator confirmation. Payment sits in on-chain escrow until approved.

The critical context: this is built by OTOY Inc., a Los Angeles company founded by Jules Urbach in 2008. OTOY created OctaneRender, one of the most widely used GPU rendering engines. The Render Network is OTOY's distributed compute layer.
