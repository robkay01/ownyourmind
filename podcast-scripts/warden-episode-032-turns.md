```
What is Warden building?
```

## TURN 04 — RACHEL
```
An AI agent Layer 1. The core product is an Agentic Wallet -- natural language interface for crypto operations. You tell it what you want in plain English and AI agents handle the complexity. There's an Agent Hub, which is like an app store for Web3 AI agents -- swap agents, trading bots, research agents, portfolio management.

The technical standout is SPEX -- Statistical Proof of Execution. Instead of expensive zero-knowledge proofs, validators independently verify random samples of AI inference outputs and reach consensus. It's published as an academic paper on arXiv.
```
