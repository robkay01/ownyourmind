```
What does peaq actually do?
```

## TURN 04 — RACHEL
```
peaq is a Polkadot parachain built on Substrate, purpose-built for machines to operate as economic participants. Not humans controlling machines through apps, but machines transacting autonomously -- a car paying for its own charging, a drone selling weather data, a robot renting idle time.

The chain has native modules baked into the protocol: peaq ID for machine identity, peaq access for permissions, peaq pay for machine-to-machine micropayments, and a nanosecond-precision time synchronisation module. These aren't smart contracts -- they're built into the chain itself.
```

## TURN 18 — RACHEL
