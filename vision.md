# Own Your Mind — Vision

**The independent rating agency for decentralised AI.**

Not a news site. Not a shill sheet. Not a dashboard pretending to be objective. OYM is the place where DeAI projects get scored honestly, positions get disclosed transparently, and the reasoning is public for anyone to challenge.

---

## The Opportunity

Decentralised AI is a $15B+ sector with no trusted independent rating system. What exists today:

- **DeFiLlama** covers DeFi with objective on-chain metrics. Nothing equivalent exists for DeAI because the metrics that matter (decentralisation, code openness, governance quality) are subjective and require research, not just API calls.
- **Messari** and **Delphi Digital** publish research behind paywalls for institutional subscribers. No public scores, no consistent framework across projects.
- **0xJeff** and **0xSammy** write investment-thesis content for crypto-native audiences. No scoring system, no project accountability.
- **CoinGecko/CoinMarketCap** track price data. They don't evaluate whether a project is actually decentralised.

The gap: **nobody is rating DeAI projects on both sovereignty and investment merit, with public methodology, disclosed positions, and editorial reasoning anyone can audit.**

OYM fills that gap.

---

## What OYM Is

**Morningstar for DeAI, not DeFiLlama for DeAI.**

DeFiLlama's value is real-time objective on-chain data. OYM's value is researched subjective assessment backed by transparent methodology. These are fundamentally different products.

Morningstar rates mutual funds with star ratings. The ratings are subjective but justified by published research. Fund managers care about their Morningstar rating because institutional allocators use it. The ratings become an industry standard that shapes behaviour.

OYM's dual-score system works the same way:

- **Freedom Score** (0–100): How decentralised is this project, really? Six dimensions, scored with evidence.
- **Returns Score** (0–100): How well does the token capture value? Five dimensions, scored with evidence.
- **Quadrant model**: A (High Freedom + High Returns — best of both), B (High Freedom + Low Returns — sovereignty plays), C (Low Freedom + High Returns — centralised value), D (Low Freedom + Low Returns — avoid). Now visible on the dashboard, project pages, and comparison tool.

The editorial reviews are not decoration. They are the proof of work behind each score. Every number has a paragraph explaining why. That's what makes the scores defensible and trustworthy.

## What Makes OYM Unique

### 1. The dual-score system
Nobody else scores both sovereignty and investment merit independently. Most coverage either ignores decentralisation (treating "DeAI" as a marketing label) or ignores returns (treating sovereignty as sufficient). OYM says: both matter, and they're often in tension. That tension is the interesting part.

### 2. Honest disclosure
Every project review states whether Rob holds the token. Every comparison article discloses positions. This is rare in crypto media and it builds trust faster than any marketing.

### 3. The construction PM lens
Rob evaluates DeAI projects the way he evaluates infrastructure assets: delivery risk, governance quality, team capability, financial sustainability. Twenty years of owner-side project management on half-billion-dollar assets creates an analytical framework that crypto-native researchers don't have. "What's the delivery risk?" is a better question than "What's the narrative?"

### 4. Agent Zero as research engine
The Agent Zero partnership enables research velocity that a solo operation normally can't achieve. Deep-dive project reviews that would take a human researcher weeks can be produced in hours, with Claude Code handling implementation. This is a structural advantage, not just a convenience.

### 5. Public methodology
Both scoring methodologies are published on the site. Anyone can read exactly how scores are calculated, challenge the reasoning, and hold OYM accountable. Transparency is the product.

## What OYM Is Not

- **Not a news site.** We don't chase daily headlines. Reviews and scores are updated on evidence, not on news cycles.
- **Not a shill sheet.** Positions are disclosed. Scores are independent of holdings. If a project Rob holds scores poorly, the score stays.
- **Not a VC research desk.** We're public-first. The methodology is open. The reasoning is visible. No paywalled alpha.
- **Not a community/social platform.** OYM is editorial, not social. The value is in the quality of assessment, not the volume of discussion.
- **Not trying to replace DeFiLlama.** Real-time on-chain metrics are their strength. Researched qualitative assessment is ours. Complementary, not competitive.
