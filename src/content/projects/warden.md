## What it does

Warden Protocol is a purpose-built Layer-1 blockchain for AI agents in Web3. It positions itself as the "distribution layer for the agent economy" – infrastructure for autonomous AI agents to execute transactions, manage assets, and interact across chains.

Core components:

- **Agentic Wallet** – natural language interface for crypto operations. "The wallet anyone can use – just ask." Agents handle the complexity behind conversational commands.
- **Agent Hub** – an app-store-style marketplace for Web3 AI agents. Pre-built agents include swap agents, trading bots (Kaibot), the Messari research agent, and portfolio management agents.
- **SPEX (Statistical Proof of Execution)** – verifiable AI inference using probabilistic sampling and consensus across validators. Published as an academic paper (arXiv: 2503.18899). Validators independently verify random samples of inference outputs rather than re-running entire computations.
- **AVRs (Asynchronous Verifiable Resources)** – plugins that encapsulate AI functions callable cross-chain. Think microservices for agent capabilities.
- **AI Trading Terminal** – Messari Signals integration for perpetuals and tokenised stocks.

Multi-chain support via IBC (Cosmos origins), Hyperlane, and deBridge. Covers Solana, Ethereum, BNB Chain, Base, Arbitrum, and WardenChain.

The project spawned from work at **Qredo** (which separately became Open Custody Protocol). The team is heavy on Qredo and Binance alumni:

- **David Pinger** (Co-Founder, Ecosystem) – LMU Munich + Pantheon Sorbonne, ex-Uber, ex-Binance, led R&D at Qredo Labs.
- **Antonio Pitasi** (Co-Founder, Protocol) – ex-Tendermint/Ignite, Cosmos SDK expertise.
- **Alex Melikhov** (Co-Founder, Product) – CEO of Equilibrium (EOSDT stablecoin).
- **Jon Heywood** (Co-Founder, Design) – 15+ years tech, ex-Qredo, built SpaceWard.
- **Josh Goodbody** (CEO) – ex-Binance, ex-Qredo.
- **Dr. Michele Dallachiesa** (Chief AI Officer) – ex-Google, ex-NASA.

Entity based in Madrid, Spain. Total funding approximately $5M: angel/seed (~$450K, early 2024), Fjord Foundry IDO ($534K at $460M FDV, April-May 2024), and strategic round ($4M at $200M valuation, January 2026 from 0G, Messari, Venice.AI).

Token launched 4 February 2026 – this project is approximately one month old at the time of writing.

## Value proposition

SPEX is the technical standout. Rather than requiring expensive ZK proofs or running every computation on-chain, SPEX uses statistical sampling – validators independently verify random samples of AI inference outputs, and consensus determines correctness. This is a practical middle ground between full verification and trust-based oracles.

The 2025 activity numbers are notable: 60.4 million AI agent runs, 11.7 million inference proofs, 10.9 million cumulative on-chain transactions, $749K protocol revenue, and $704K agent revenue. Those are real on-chain metrics.

The counter-narrative is significant:

**Inflated user numbers.** Warden claimed 15-20 million users. The founders then admitted to purging approximately 6 million bots. The real active user count is unclear. When a project's first instinct is to trumpet inflated numbers and then quietly walk them back, that warrants scepticism about all their metrics.

**93% crash in one month.** WARD launched at TGE on 4 February 2026 at $0.14 ATH and sits at ~$0.01 as of early March 2026. Fjord Foundry IDO participants are down 97.8%. That is a brutal one-month performance.

**Proprietary core module.** The x/async module – a key piece of infrastructure – is proprietary with restricted use. The rest of the codebase is Apache 2.0, but having a core component closed-source undermines the open-source narrative.

**42% insider allocation.** Core contributors (20%) + treasury/R&D (22%) = 42% controlled by the team. With 120M treasury tokens unlocked at genesis and only 25% of supply circulating, significant sell pressure lies ahead.

**Unlimited max supply.** PoS inflation between 1-10% annually, targeting 65% staked. The 3% community tax burn on fees provides modest deflationary pressure but on $750K annual revenue, the burn is negligible compared to inflation.

The "Do-It-For-Me Economy" framing – agents as autonomous economic actors – is compelling as a vision but describes a future that doesn't exist yet.

## Tokenomics

WARD has a genesis supply of 1 billion tokens with unlimited maximum supply due to PoS inflation (1-10% annually). Approximately 250 million are circulating (25%).

Distribution: core contributors 20% (6-month cliff + 24-month linear), treasury/R&D 22% (120M at genesis + 12-month linear), agent incentives 19% (20M at genesis + 12-month linear), ecosystem/community 12% (20M at genesis + 30-month linear), validators 10% (permanently locked), public goods 5.3%, liquidity 5%, initial airdrop 4.7%, strategic investors 2% (12-month cliff + 12-month linear).

Token utility: gas on WardenChain, PoS staking, governance voting, agent execution fees, operator bonding. A 3% community tax on protocol fees is burned programmatically – the sole deflationary mechanism.

WARD trades at approximately $0.01 with a market cap of ~$2.5M and FDV of ~$9.9M. Daily volume $2.6-4.6M – notably exceeding the market cap, suggesting active speculation. Listed on KuCoin, Bybit, BingX, Bitget, and Binance Alpha. Available on WardenChain, BNB Chain, and Base.

**Build Agents.** Use Warden Studio to create and deploy AI agents. SPEX for verifiable inference. Technical skill: advanced.
