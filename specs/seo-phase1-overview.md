# SEO Phase 1 — Overview

5 content enrichment tasks for Agent Zero. All are optimisations to existing published pages, not new project research. The full 8-step research protocol does NOT apply — these are keyword-targeted content additions to pages that already have complete reviews.

## What A0 needs to do

For each task: read the existing page content, research the specific topic, write new sections in Rob's voice that slot into the existing editorial structure. Do NOT rewrite existing content — add to it.

## Writing voice

UK English. Practitioner voice. Australian directness. Construction PM lens. No marketing speak. See `rob-profile.md` for full voice guide.

## Quality bar

- Every claim must be verifiable (link source or cite on-chain data)
- Scores must NOT change — these are SEO enrichments, not re-scoring
- New sections must match the tone and depth of existing content
- Target: 300-600 words per new section (enough for SEO value, not padding)
